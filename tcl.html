TCL bans - Server admin setup guide
How it works
  •  Bans are added by the TCL leadership.
  •  Our bot messages each guilds admin team asking if they would like to ban the account on their own server.
  •  Our bot will then ban the user.
Setup actions
  •  Create a discord channel called tcl-bot.
  •  Add our bot
    TCL Bans
     Add bot
  •  Give the bot permission to read & write messages into the channel called tcl-bot.
  •  Give the bot permission to Manage bans on your server.
